Typographic Styling

Bullets:

Inset from body copy left margins. Can use any color from palette.

Phone numbers:

(000) 000-0000

Dates:

December XX, XXXX

Web addresses:

ColonialLife.com (do not include www.)

Percent or %:

Use % symbol in call-outs. Spell out percent in body copy.

Numbers:

Spell out number one through ten in body copy. Use numerals for 11 and greater.
