Typographic Styling

Bullets:

Inset from body copy left margins. Can use any color from palette.

Phone numbers:

(000) 000-0000

Dates:

December XX, XXXX

Web addresses:

ColonialLife.com (do not include www.)

Percent or %:

Use % symbol in call-outs. Spell out percent in body copy.

Numbers:

Spell out number one through ten in body copy. Use numerals for 11 and greater.

Grid System

Colonial Life's visual brand is expressed in a grid system. The boxes within the system are employed to bring emphasis and importance to the content.

Boxes

May use any color of the primary palette. Secondary and Accent palettes may only be used for visual emphasis and never be a dominate element.
